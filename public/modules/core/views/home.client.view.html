About McKenzie Tobeck

McKenzie has been competitively showing Arabian and Half-Arabian horses for fourteen years and has garnered multiple national championships and reserve championships in the youth, amateur, and open divisions. She will be specializing in English and Country English Pleasure, ShowHack, Hunter, and Western Pleasure horses.

McKenzie is also passionate about brining more riders into the Arabian Horse Association and is starting a lesson program for riders of all ages and abilities.

After growing up in the horse industry and with many years of experience successfully training, marketing, showing, and teaching lessons, McKenzie is passionate about bringing horses and riders together and helping people achieve their dreams. The future of Tobeck Performance Horses is bright!
