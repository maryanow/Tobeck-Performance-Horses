About

Sitting at the base of Mount Rainier, Journeywood Farms is centrally located in Eatonville, Washington about fifty minutes south east of Seattle and Seattle-Tacoma International Airport, as well as roughly two hours from the Portland airport.

The farm is fifteen acres in total consisting of three large pastures; five run style turnouts, forty-four stalls, round pen and an indoor arena. With round-the-clock care, the horses at Journeywood Farms enjoy a nice mixture of first-class indoor and outdoor care that meets the needs of each horse.
